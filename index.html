Bid on a Noun
Every Noun is one vote, and every day one Noun is born and sold to the highest bidder. Below you'll find a series of auction clients where you can place a bid on todays Noun or check out previous auctions.
↓
Name Description
Nouns.wtf The original frontend client was built by the Nouns founders. While some aspects of this site are deprecated, bidding still works great. However, consider using one of the clients below, created by builders who earn rewards when used through Client ID incentives.
Probe.wtf A simple bidding interface, while allowing users to explore more info on each Noun.
NounSwap.wtf Bid, swap, and explore Nouns through Noun Swap. This client allows you to not only bid, but explore Nouns held in the treasury and $nouns pool. They've made it easy to swap 1M $nouns for a Noun.
Pronouns.gg View or bid on current auction. Breaks down traits, and trait floor for each Noun. Also displays book value and %-change from previous auction.
Nouns.sh Nouns Terminal: Data packed Nouns auction client letting users see additional details on bidders and their bids.
shenounigans.live Daily auctions with live feed of on-chain actions via the "auctioneer," with a live chat. Traits are displayed alongside each Noun.
Proposals (Read, Vote, Write)
Nearly every day a new idea is added as Candidate Proposal or fully as an onchain Proposal. A great way to get into Nouns is to read, provide feedback, and vote on active proposals even if you dont own a Noun~ Have a great idea yourself? Add it as a Candidate proposal.
↓
Name Description
Nouns.Game New: Play Nouns at Nouns.Game, an action packed client with "quick vote" and expanding feature set. When a proposal is live, explore the "lobby" to see who hasnt voted yet: "nouns.game/lobby/_prop_id_".
Nouns.Camp Popular: Streamline governance interface for voters and proposers. Read and provide feedback on candidate proposals. Users have the ability to re-vote or "quote" other peoples reasons in their own votes or "signals." Additional details on voters and their voting history are available to explore here.
Nouns Agora Frontend client built and maintained by the Agora team. Read proposals, view vote reasons, and find out more information on voters and their voting habits.
Noun.wtf 🚧 Out of Order: The original frontend created by the founders is still a good place to read proposals, but it is currently not working for voting. Please use one of the other frontends below to vote on proposals.
Propdates.wtf A place for builders who have passed proposals to leave onchain public updates for the DAO.
Proposals.wtf A Proposal tracker built by long time community member Maty who manually tracks and documents progress on each proposal. This is a great source of up-to-date data on proposal progress, funds and admin details.
Community
There are a ton of ways to get involved in the Nouns community or meet other Nouns contributors. Below you'll find suggestions on where to begin and ways you can contribute on a regular basis.
↓
Name Description
Warpcast Warpcast is a onchain social media platform where Nouns has a dedicated channel. You can find conversation around Nouns primarily in /nouns. Join in on the conversation and don't be afraid to ask questions ⌐◨-◨
TNS:
Noun O' Clock Live		 The Noun Square hosts a daily show as the Noun auction ends and a new Noun is born. They congratulate the winner, talk about Nouns and attempt to settle a new Noun each day: Sometimes based on requests! Join in on the fun and keep up with Nouns at Noun O' Clock Live! Join in daily on Tavern.
FOMO Nouns Played alongside The Noun Square's Noun O' Clock Live, "FOMO Nouns" is a game developed to help pick the upcoming Noun. As random Nouns appear, users vote up up or down new Nouns in an attempt to create one through group consensus. Join in on the fun 7-days a week!
Nouncil Nouncil is a body of Nouns participants who gather to review and collectively vote on proposals together. Meetings are every Thursdays at 10am EST. This call in open to the public. Anyone can attend, listen, provide feedback and apply to become a Nouncil member. Builders who have upcoming or live proposals are invited to speak: But please ask to be added to the schedule ahead of time—The calls can get full quickly. Nouncil is delegated Nouns and anyone can apply for membership. Find out more info in the Nouncil Discord.
Zero Weight Vote on Nouns proposals through Zero Weight on Warpcast and have weight thrown behind the most popular stance: Type +FOR, +AGAINST, +ABSTAIN to cast your vote with or without reason.
Rounds.wtf Create or contribute to Rounds: Rounds is an easy and fun way for communities to reward contributors. It transforms traditional bounty or grant programs into engaging, interactive experiences that involve the entire community. Through rounds, you can incentivize activity in any category to activate and grow your community. Explore ways to contribute to Nouns and earn via Rounds.wtf.
Uplink.wtf Create or join into community contests: Join individuals, brands, and decentralized orgs using Uplink to organize onchain and co-create masterpieces with their most passionate supporters. Visit Uplink.wtf to explore ways to contribute and earn.
Noun Scout Want to see a specific Noun head or trait minted? Noun Scout allows anyone to put up a reward for minting a Noun with a specific trait and donates the funds to a good cause.
Noggles Reward tokens for specific Nounish projects based on governance actions. Buy, earn, tip and spend Nogs in various ways across the Nouns Ecosystem. Own a Noun, Lil Noun, Gnar or Yellow Collective Noun? — You may already be earning Nogs!
Nouns Name Service (NNS) Nouns Name Service: Attach the legendary ⌐◨-◨ to your web3 identity. Similar to ens names on ethereum, nns allows users to buy and user .⌐◨-◨ eth addresses which show up on pages like Nouns.wtf.
BuyNouns.xyz Explore the $noun market and how close to one million $noun (one full Noun) people currently have.
NounSpace A fun configurable and customizable Nounish "home page". Users can create and share their homepage for others to visit and interact with.
Art Resources
Nouns art is CC0. This means it was created and released directly into the Public Domain. Nouns art is free to use, no permission required. Because of this artists have created a sea of resources for others to use in their own work, or to design new Nouns traits. Explore some of these below.
↓
Name Description
Nouns Figma Figma file with all of the Nouns traits split up by category.
Noundry Studio Nouns pixel studio that allows you to create and export Nouns traits using the correct colors and exporting at standard sizes for Nouns inclusion.
Noundry Gallery After you've designed and exported a new Nouns trait from Noundry Studio, upload it to Noundry Gallery. Noundry Gallery is a place to add newly created traits and share them for feedback. You can also use the website to create a proposal for your trait to be included in the Nouns collection.
Probe.wtf Explore Nouns with Probe.wtf: A Nouns explorer that lets you dig into each Noun, and the overall Nouns art, and filter by various characteristcs, not limited to traits.
CCO-Lib A repository of cc0 Nouns content made easily accessable for users to search.
Noundation Excellent design resource and Noggle bank. Check out the endless variations of unicode noggles and some killer design resources.
Palettes.wtf An exploration of color theory based on the official Nouns color palette, created to inspire designers and builders in and outside of the Nouns ecosystem 🎨
Nouns Canvas A repository of Nounish design resources. This site compiles multiple sources you can use to start designing with Nouns.
Nouns on Giphy There are a ton of Nouns related GIFs and images on Giphy. Looking for something fun and Nounish to share? Look no further.
3D Nouns Fun resource that allows users to generate, animate, and export 3D Nouns files! Have fun!
